Press <strong>Unlock/Lock</strong> to unlock and relock the query window. Before submitting, you are encouraged to first press <strong>Check Syntax</strong> to ensure your query is valid. Then, hit <strong>Submit</strong> to begin your query. Once finished, your results will be displayed in the column on the right and, if you check the <strong>Open in New Tab</strong> button, a new tab. At this point, you can either <strong>Download</strong> your query results to a .csv file or <strong>Reset</strong> your results in preparation for another query.
